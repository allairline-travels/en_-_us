🆘 Corsair Airlines Customer Service – Ask for Help

If you need assistance with your booking, flight, or any travel-related issue, Corsair Airlines offers multiple support options. Reach out based on your location and preference. 🌐

📞 Phone Support

You can ask for help by calling the following numbers:

  • 🇫🇷 From France: Dial 3917 (€0.35/min from landline)
  • 🌍 International: +1-888-829-1117
  • 🇺🇸 North America: +1 888-657-8380

🏢 Local Office Contacts

For regional support, you can reach our offices in the U.S.:

  • 🗽 New York: +1 888-657-8380
  • 🌴 Los Angeles: +1 888-657-8380

📧 Email Help

Prefer written communication? Email your inquiries to corsair.res@apg-ga.ca

✍️ Be sure to include: your full name, booking reference, flight details, and a clear explanation of your issue for faster support.

🕐 Customer Service Hours

France: Daily from 7:00 AM – 11:00 PM (local time)

North America: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:30 PM (local time)

🛬 Airport Help Desks

Need immediate help in person? Visit the Corsair customer service counter at your departure airport for assistance with:

  • Flight changes
  • Cancellations
  • Baggage issues
  • Check-in support
✅ Bring your booking reference and a valid ID to speed up service.
